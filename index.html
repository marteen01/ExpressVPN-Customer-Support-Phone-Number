Is There an ExpressVPN Customer Support Phone Number?

Currently, ExpressVPN does not offer a public phone number for customer service. This may seem unusual in a world where phone support is common, but ExpressVPN has chosen to provide support through more efficient and secure channels such as live chat and email.

Why No Phone Number?

There are several reasons why ExpressVPN has opted out of traditional phone support:

  • Speed: Live chat is often faster and more effective than waiting on hold.
  • Security: Phone support can be a target for social engineering; digital channels are more secure.
  • Documentation: Written support (chat/email) allows clear records of all communication.
  • Efficiency: Agents can assist multiple users at once via chat, reducing wait times for everyone.

How to Contact ExpressVPN Support

The fastest and easiest way to reach ExpressVPN is through their 24/7 live chat. Available at any time, it connects you with a real support agent in less than a minute. Whether you need help installing the VPN, resolving errors, or managing your account, chat support is always available.

2. Email Support

For more complex queries or if you need to attach screenshots or logs, email support is available. You can describe your issue in detail and expect a reply within a few hours. This option is especially helpful for technical troubleshooting or billing concerns.

3. Help Center & Tutorials

Before contacting support, it’s recommended to check out the Help Center. It contains setup guides, troubleshooting articles, FAQs, and tutorials for every supported device and platform. You can often find your answer without waiting for an agent.

Tips for Faster Support

To get the fastest resolution when reaching out to support, consider including:

  • Your registered email address or username

  • Steps you’ve already tried

Are There Other Contact Methods?
